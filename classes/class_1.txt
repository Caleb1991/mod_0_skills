class Kitchen

attributes:
number_of_chefs (integer)
is_stove_on (boolean)
order_numbers (array)
dirty_dishes_in_sink (boolean)

methods:
clock_in_chef (increases number_of_chefs by one)
turn_on_stove (sets is_stove_on to true)
take_order (adds an integer to order_numbers)
bring_back_dishes (sets dirty_dishes_in_sink to true)
